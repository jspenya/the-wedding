Dress Code

Garden Formal

We encourage you to dress elegantly while keeping in mind our outdoor garden setting. Think romantic, natural, and refined.

Suggested for Ladies

  • Formal dresses or gowns
  • Light colors and floral patterns
  • Avoid stiletto heels (grass!)

  • Ties optional but encouraged
