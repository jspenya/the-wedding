Dress Code

Garden Formal

We encourage you to dress elegantly while keeping in mind our outdoor garden setting. Think romantic, natural, and refined.

Suggested for Ladies

  • Formal dresses or gowns
  • Light colors and floral patterns
  • Avoid stiletto heels (grass!)
  • Consider a light wrap for evening

Suggested for Gentlemen

  • Suits or dress slacks with blazer
  • Ties optional but encouraged
  • Light colors welcome
  • Dress shoes appropriate for grass

Parking & Accessibility

Parking Information

Free parking is available on-site with designated spaces for guests. Parking attendants will be available to direct you. Overflow parking is available in the adjacent lot, just a short walk to the venue.

Accessibility

The venue is wheelchair accessible with designated parking spaces near the entrance. Accessible seating will be available for the ceremony and reception. Please let us know if you have specific accessibility needs.
